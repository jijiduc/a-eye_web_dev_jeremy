Frequently Asked Questions

I didn't receive the activation link. How do I proceed?

First, please check your spam. Second, if you did not receive the email, you can use the forgot password feature on the login page to get back an activation link.

Is it possible to submit DICOM images directly?

No, the currently only supported formats are compressed Nifti: ".nii.gz" and .zip. The zip can include DICOM or Nifti images, and several images if needed. When you upload a zip, the content is extracted, and the DICOM images are converted into Nifti (.nii.gz). Please read the tutorial for help on format conversion. You can have additional help watching this video or reading this book.

The pipeline finished, but I have an issue on the obtained results. How to report a bug?

To report an issue on the platform or the pipelines, please contact: jaime.barrancohernandez@hevs.ch.

Where is my medical data stored and is it exposed?

Imaging data is temporarily stored on a secured physical server at the HES-SO in Sion (Valais) and is kept for improving A-eye's models performance. Your images and our outputs are really useful for us for continuos integration and development (CI/CD) :)

How can I reset my password if I forgot it?

If you forgot your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your registered email address, and a password reset link will be sent to you. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

I can't create an account.

We allow our partners, universities, and various institutions to use our web platform. If you are unable to create an account, it is possible that your email address contains a domain name that is unknown to us. If you believe you are eligible to use the web platform and are unable to create an account, please contact A-eye web platform support at the following address: jaime.barrancohernandez@hevs.ch
